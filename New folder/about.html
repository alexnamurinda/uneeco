Our Story

Three Decades of Print Excellence

From a small operation to East Africa's leading paper supplier — here's how we got here.

Founded with Purpose

We Began with a Simple Mission

Uneeco Paper Products Ltd. was founded with a clear vision: to bring world-class printing materials to East African businesses and to partner with them in growing the region's graphic arts industry. Starting in the early 1990s in Nairobi's Industrial Area, we set out to become more than a supplier — we aimed to be a true partner in print.

Over three decades, we have built an extensive portfolio of exclusively represented global brands, grown to serve clients across Kenya, Uganda, and the wider East Africa region, and developed a reputation built on integrity, expertise, and genuine commitment to customer success.

Today, UNEECO is widely recognised as the one-stop shop for the entire graphic arts industry — serving offset printers, digital print shops, label converters, packaging manufacturers, and more.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1990s

Founded in Nairobi

Uneeco Paper Products Ltd. established in Nairobi's Industrial Area with a focus on supplying quality printing papers to the local market.

Expanded operations to Mombasa and began serving clients across the wider East African market, cementing our regional presence.
