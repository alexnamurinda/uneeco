1984 Established
Our Story

Established in 1984, Uneeco has grown at an unprecedented pace to firmly establish itself as one of the leading suppliers to the Print and Packaging industry in East Africa. Now successfully operating for 30+ years in the region, we have built a vast knowledge and expertise on print media — and yet we couldn't be more excited about what the future holds for print.

With large warehousing facilities of over 15,000 sq metres in various East African cities, we pride ourselves in being the 'one stop shop' for the entire graphic arts industry irrespective of print technology.

We have a market leading range of over 8,000 products including a wide range of paper and packaging substrates, promotional and visual communicational material as well as print machinery, inks, plates, chemicals and various other printing consumables — all backed up with service and support that is second to none.

Whether you're running a large commercial printing organisation, graphic design studio or advertising agency, a converting (packaging) company, a reprographic house / trade shop, or a small office, we have something to offer your business and help you get the best from your paper based communication, whilst adding value to your supply chain.

Here at Uneeco, we continually strive to provide our clients with innovative technological solutions to print and production challenges.
